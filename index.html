Making a Difference :
Our Initiatives

Medical treatment

Assessments, vaccinations, surgeries, and ongoing care for their well-being.

Health Checkups

Providing vital health checkups to stray animals, ensuring their well-being through assessments, vaccinations, and necessary medical interventions for a healthier future.

Rehabilitation

Our dedicated rehabilitation program focuses on nurturing and supporting injured or traumatized animals. Through specialized care, therapy, and love, we strive to restore their physical health and emotional well-being, enabling them to thrive and find happiness once again.

Adoption & Awareness

Fostering adoption and spreading awareness for animal welfare through connecting rescued animals with loving homes and educating communities on responsible pet ownership and compassionate practices

Rescue ops.

Rescuing and providing love to senior dogs, ensuring they receive the care and comfort they deserve in their golden years.
